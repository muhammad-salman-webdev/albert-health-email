MAY 2025

Introducing the New Albert Health

A bold new identity. A stronger vision.

A deeper commitment to empowering patients with chronic conditions.

We’re excited to introduce the new Albert Health, the result of an in-depth transformation shaped by real insights from patients, healthcare professionals and strategic partners, and guided by the experiences of over 300,000 patients we’ve supported throughout their health journeys.

 

This shift represents more than a new visual direction. It reflects a deeper evolution in how we think, design, communicate, and deliver healthcare, enabling patients to feel more informed, more supported, and more in control of their health..

 

At every stage of this transformation, we worked hand-in-hand with Landor, a global brand consultancy and design agency with decades of experiencebuilding some of the world’s most iconic brands, to create a renewed identity built on clarity, trust, and purpose..

 

As part of this transformation, the Albert Health Assistant app has also been rebuilt, offering faster performance, enhanced data security, and a more intuitive, personalized experience for managing chronic conditions.
